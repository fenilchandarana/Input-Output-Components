hall effect sensor		UNO

GND			GND
VCC			VCC
DO			digital pin
or
AO			analog pin


if you connect AO of hall effect sensor to the analog pin of the UNO, the reading will go from 1023 to 71 if there is magnet on top. Reading will be either 1023 or 71 for analog reading. 
if you connect DO of hall effect sensor to the digital pin of the UNO, the reading will go from 1 to 0.
